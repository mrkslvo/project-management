  • 1. Unique QR Codes: Each student is provided with a unique QR code that can be scanned for attendance.
  • 2. QR Code Scanning: Instructors or students can scan the QR codes using a smartphone or tablet to register attendance.
  • 3. Time Stamping: The system records the exact date and time when the QR code is scanned, ensuring accurate tracking of attendance.
  • 4. Real-Time Updates: Attendance records are updated in real-time and can be accessed by both students and teachers instantly.
  • 5. Automated Reporting: The system generates automated attendance reports, which can be reviewed by instructors and administrators.
  • 6. Notifications: Automatic notifications can be sent to students who are absent or late.
  • 7. Integration with Learning Management Systems (LMS): The attendance data can be integrated with existing LMS platforms for streamlined record-keeping and analysis.
  • 8. Security Features: Measures to prevent fraudulent attendance marking, such as QR codes that change periodically or are valid only for a specific time window.
  • 9. User-Friendly Interface: An intuitive interface for both teachers and students to use the system without extensive training.
  • 10. Analytics: Tools to analyze attendance patterns and generate insights, such as identifying students with frequent absences.
